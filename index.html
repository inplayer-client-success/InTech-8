Welcome

What can you expect from InTech Meetup #8?

InTech meetup #8 has a new virtual format and a profound cause: building the first Golang community in North Macedonia. At InPlayer we use Golang to build our amazing platform and we’d like to share our love for this technology with everyone. Golang, as a robust system-level language used for programming across large-scale network servers and big distributed systems, its features and applications, especially in the context of cloud computing and infrastructure is something that sparks interest and high level of curiosity among the software engineering community in general.


That indicated, InTech Meetup is the perfect opportunity to start dwelling deeper into “All things Go” and we are excited to invite all the tech enthusiasts to our 8th in a row InTech Meetup which will take place on the 19th of May (Wednesday) at 6 PM CET.

Speakers

Speakers InTech Meetup #8

WILLIAM KENNEDY

Keynote Speaker

William Kennedy is a managing partner at Ardan Labs in Miami, Florida, a mobile, web, and systems development company. He is also a co-author of the book Go in Action, the author of the blog GoingGo.Net, and a founding member of GoBridge which is working to increase Go adoption through diversity.

William will give us an insightful presentation on the topic : “Profiling and Tracing Go Apps”. William will aid us understand how to use the profile and trace tooling in Go. We will use a practical program to find performance problems and based on what we find, refactor code to gain better performance.

NATALIE PISTUNOVICH

Keynote Speaker

Natalie is a learner, a Google Developer Expert for Go, an OpenAI ambassador, a public speaker and a sailor. When she's not working on robust systems at Aerospike, she is organizing the GopherCon Europe and Cloud Nein conferences, and the Berlin chapters of the Go and Women Techmakers user groups. Previously, she was an Engineering Manager, Software and Hardware Engineer, and a Co-Founder of a mobile start-up. In her free time, she is wondering if there is life on Mars...

Natalie, joining us from Berlin, will share knowledge and expertise on the topic: “Infrastructure for ML Applications”. Via her presentation she will facilitate the process of getting a close glimpse on how to robustly build a Machine Learning system and how to build a service that interfaces with a fast secondary data source to enhance the model’s predictions. We will have a chance to inspect infrastructure, model serving/deployment and data enrichment separately and create a working stack together.

Stojan Dimitrovski

Keynote Speaker

Stojan is a life-long learner, engineer, hacker and tinkerer. He likes to deep dive into various programming languages and technologies and tries to find great uses for them. In his free time he likes to read and invest in stocks.

Stojan will dwell into the topic Goroutines : The machine behind the machine. Ever wondered how Goroutines actually work and what actually makes them so fast, light and efficient? In this talk we take a dive behind the scenes exploring all the technology that makes Goroutines happen.

About Us

What is an InTech Meetup?

InTech Meetup is all about knowledge and experience exchange in terms of cutting-edge technologies and networking among the tech community in North Macedonia. Made with love and powered by the InPlayer team, our goal is to establish a place where people from all backgrounds interested in new and emerging technologies can meet, share experiences, learn, present, discuss and network.

We’ve been organizing InTech since 2016 and we are proud of all the renowned local and international speakers who have been part of it, covering a variety of topics such as Microservices, Tech & Gaming, Celebration of the 20th anniversary Open Source Software, Machine Learning and many more.

We encourage everyone who is interested to contribute or participate in the InTech meetups to drop us a line at intech@inplayer.com and help grow the community even stronger.
